Meet your host

Aramide Adesulu

As a child I was raised Presbyterian, but growing up rejected Christianity due to its history in regard to the African continent. For about 2 or 3 years I turned away from institutionalized religion and spirituality in its entirety. After some time, I restarted my spiritual journey in 2020 after a friend introduced to me the concept of human beings emitting frequencies wavelengths and energies. My experience further grew at that time during a former relationship. While in the relationship, my then partner expressed his ideas about the institution of religion (Christianity & Islam) while introducing me to the concept of the traditional Yoruba Religion, Ifa. From then on, my mind opened to the various concepts of spirituality in general, varying from astrology to interest in the Quran.


One of my favorite quotes reads, "Religion is like the peel of a banana while spirituality is the fruit. Most of us hold on to the peel, sadly." - Some guy on the internet

About Us

There are many conflicting ideas regarding the concept of spirituality. What is it? Who’s right who’s wrong, who knows, who even cares? What we are here to do is invite you on a journey of learning, with us, the various dogmas, ideas, and practices pertaining to spirituality. We, like you, want to invoke mental, emotional and spiritual growth to become the best versions of ourselves. We hope that you take some things from here and find your own spiritual path.


I personally am a novice when it comes to spirituality, I’m learning a lot of these concepts right along with you guys. This Podcast was made to help with my spiritual journey as well, in effort to become a more compassionate (loving myself and people around me, reducing judgement, and obtaining a sense of oneness with the world around me (we are all connected)) and purposeful (living with purpose and intention) individual. Join me, let’s learn, fail, learn again and grow together
